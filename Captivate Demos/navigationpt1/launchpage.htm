Objectives
Once you've completed this module, you will be able to:

  • Log in and log out of MFP.
  • Understand what is meant by the Task Pane and the elements within it.
  • Select the data to use and create a new workbook.
  • Change the level of the hierarchy.
  • Change the view of data on screen.
  • Commit a workbook and understand the Commit Status.
