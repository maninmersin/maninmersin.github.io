Creating a Quick Part in Word 2010

Quick Parts is a gallery where you can create, store, and find reusable pieces of content. It’s the facility to save standard bits of content so you can recycle them to use over and over again saving you time and effort.


For example you can save your company contact details or maybe you have a standard cover sheet that you would like to be easily available for inserting.

This module shows you how to create and save a Quick Part.
