The eLearning modules provide you with instruction about how to use the system. You can practice a task in a simulated environment and you can also test your knowledge of a task.

Modes

See It! - sit back and watch
Learn by watching an animated demonstration of tasks being performed in a simulated environment. All the required activities, such as moving the mouse and entering data, are completed automatically.
Don't worry if you don't catch all the text - just watch the actions, then Try It!
Try It! - be guided through each task
This mode enables you to learn interactively in a simulated environment. The Player provides you with instructions as to what needs to be done to complete a task, but you perform the actual steps.
Know It? - show what you've learned
This mode enables you to test knowledge gained from training. You are instructed to complete the steps for a particular task and are scored on how accurately you complete the steps.

Details of where to get help and support when using the system

We want to help resolve your issues and queries as quickly as possible.

What do we need from you?

  • We need you to use this process to log all your issues and queries with us as quickly as possible, providing as much information as possible
  • By following this process you will be able to contact the right team and they will be able to resolve your issue or query in the most effective way
  • By logging your issue with us, you’ll enable us to see where the same issues are occurring across departments and across stores.
    This will enable us to address the root cause of issues and prevent the same issues happening in the future

What’s a technical issue?

Examples of queries that are Technical Issues:

  • The screen takes a lot longer than normal to be displayed
  • When entering data into field or selecting an option on the screen it takes a lot longer than normal for the screen to update
  • You are unable to log into IMS
  • IMS closes by itself
  • The screen freezes when you are using IMS

I’ve identified I have a technical issue. What do I do?

Contact the IT Service Desk by emailing xxx@aggreko.com. Remember to provide the IT Service Desk with as much information as possible. 

Best practice for logging an issue with the IT Service Desk

  • Always log your issue
    • If it’s a recurring issue, you need to log it each time it happens, not just the first time (unless the Service Desk Advise/Data Support you others)
    • Seeing the frequency of the issue raised will help give us a sense of the severity and business impact
  • One issue, one ticket
    • Don’t consolidate multiple items on one ticket
  • Provide as much information as possible including:
    • Time
    • Transaction number
    • Location
    • Screen shots
    • If it’s slow, how much longer is it taking (in minutes)?
  • Ask for the Freshdesk ticket number when you log your call
    • This will help you or the Project team follow up on your call
